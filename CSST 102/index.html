FAQ

How much math is required?

Comfort with algebra and basic probability is enough. We focus on intuition and practice.

Do I need a powerful laptop?

No. Colab notebooks run in the cloud, and datasets are modest. Any recent laptop is fine.

Will we cover deep learning?

Only conceptually. The emphasis is on classic, widely applicable methods for tabular data.
